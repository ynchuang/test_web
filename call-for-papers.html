Topics of Interest

We are soliciting paper submissions on all topics related to the interface of artificial intelligence and statistics, with a particular focus on problems motivated by applications.

Foundation models, generative models and statistical perspectives (language models, diffusion models, etc.)

Improving the reliability of AI through statistical methods (diagnostics, uncertainty quantification, conformal prediction and risk control, calibration, etc.)

AI evaluation

Interventions and experiment design for machine learning and AI

Applications of data-driven methodology to scientific and business areas, including the biomedical sciences, social sciences, physical sciences, etc.

Novel software and workflow aiming to enhance researcher productivity

AI-enhanced statistical data analysis tools
