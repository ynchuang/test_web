About ICASA

Bridging the gap between statistics and artificial intelligence through application-driven research.

Enhancing Statistics with AI

The International Conference on Application-Driven Statistics and AI (ICASA) is a one-day research conference that brings together researchers, industry practitioners, and students working at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, data science, and AI.

With a focus on problems motivated by real-world applications, ICASA provides a platform for sharing innovative methods, tools, and insights that advance both statistical practice and AI capabilities.
