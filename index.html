Call for Papers

We invite researchers, industry practitioners, and students to submit their latest work. Join us in shaping the future of application-driven statistics and AI.

Paper Submission May 1, 2026

Topics of Interest

We are soliciting paper submissions on all topics related to the interface of AI and statistics, with a particular focus on problems motivated by applications.

Foundation models, generative models & statistical perspectives (language models, diffusion models, etc.)
Improving the reliability of AI through statistical methods (diagnostics, uncertainty quantification, conformal prediction, calibration, etc.)
AI evaluation
Interventions & experiment design for ML and AI
Applications of data-driven methodology to scientific & business areas (biomedical, social, physical sciences, etc.)
Novel software & workflow to enhance researcher productivity
AI-enhanced statistical data analysis tools

Paper submissions should be made at the OpenReview submission portal (link coming soon) following the submission instructions outlined at the link.

Fast track to journal acceptance: Selected papers will be considered for fast-track publication in JASA Applications & Case Studies (JASA A&CS).
